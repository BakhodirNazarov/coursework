About us

EzFlights is a power travel information aggregator

EzFlight is different from other online ticket search engine sites in that it is more of an aggregate travel-search engine than a booking engine. Instead of booking through them, EzFlights allows airlines to post to their site and be included in searches, but take bookings elsewhere.

EzFlights search engine also scans the web for prices from other booking sites like Priceline, Hotwire, Expedia. This distinctive approach gives the customer control of which option they will book with, allowing them to easily compare and find the best price available; this flexibility makes EzFlights a more popular choice among travelers.
